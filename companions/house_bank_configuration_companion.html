⚠
T030H / OBA1 — Material Financial Reporting Gap
Bank accounts holding real cash in 80+ currencies across 80+ field offices are NOT configured for month-end FX revaluation.
FAGL_FC_VAL silently skips any G/L account not present in T030H. The resulting FX gains/losses never reach the income statement.

Section A — Why OBA1 Exists: 3 FX Scenarios

Each scenario maps to specific T030H fields. All 3 must be configured for non-USD accounts.

Exchange Rate Difference — Realized

LSREA / LHREA

When: Open item is CLEARED (e.g. FEBAN clears bank statement line vs payment document)

What happens: Document posted at exchange rate X, cleared at rate Y → realized gain or loss must be booked

Needed for: ALL non-USD accounts with clearing activity (bank 10* AND clearing 11*)

Loss → 6045011    Gain → 7045011

Valuation — Month-End MOST CRITICAL

LSBEW / LHBEW

When: Month-end run of FAGL_FC_VAL

What happens: G/L balances revalued at closing rate → unrealized gain/loss posted (reversed at start of next period)

Needed for: ALL non-USD accounts holding balances — INCLUDING bank accounts (10*)

⚠ WITHOUT THIS: FAGL_FC_VAL skips the account entirely. Cash on the balance sheet is valued at the WRONG rate.

Val.loss → 6045011    Val.gain → 7045011
Reversed automatically next period

Balance Sheet Adjustment

LKORR

When: SAP posts any valuation or realized entry

What happens: Correction account for the offsetting entry to balance the posting

Standard pattern: LKORR = the account itself (e.g., account 1165424 points to 1165424)

⚠ WITHOUT THIS: SAP cannot complete the posting at all. Transaction fails with error.

Bal.sheet adj.1 → Account itself
