What Does This Transport Package Do?

These two transports together create a brand new company code STEM in SAP, representing the UNESCO China Institute. Transport 1 (JP_LOPEZ) sets up FI core: the legal entity definition, chart of accounts (541 GL accounts), house banking, payment processing, tolerance controls, asset accounting, controlling area, and invoice verification. Transport 2 (I_KONAKOV) sets up Funds Management: the FM Area, hierarchy variants, and FM-specific T001 settings.

In plain terms: after both transports are imported in order, STEM can post journal entries, manage budgets via FM, run the payment program, manage fixed assets, and process invoices.
